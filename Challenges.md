# A lot of this is advice. If you are you build this phone, PLEASE take this as advice

## Largest Challenge - Parts do not fit!
This was my largest issue with the project. Due to an incorrectly designed CAD that did not account for the modem and display wiring, everything did not fit.
If you are to take on this project, try modifying it to use a CM5 or a slimmer board as the compute or modify the cad.
My fix for this issue was Duct Tape, however due to **complications** during final assembly (Which was unathourized) the phone was wrapped in large amounts of duct tape. Requiring me to slice the entire thing open to get to the power port.
The True fix, light amounts of duct tape

## Other Issues you may encounter
### On Screen Keyboard
When I was testing the touchscreen, KDE Plasma Mobile did not show the on screen keyboard. I tried several fixes and none worked.
### Modem Not Making Calls
The modem's firmware was not compatible for making calls, so i would reccomend choosing a different modem.
### Issues with the Modem's free SIM card
Just dont use it. I tried several IMEI configs and data configs but none worked so i would reccomend getting an actual SIM card (For testing i used a Mint Mobile/T-Mobile SIM).

## OS
Personally i would reccomend either PostMarketOS or Ubuntu with Ubuntu-Desktop removed and KDE Plasma Mobile installed.
